About Me (CV)

I am currently a second year MSc. student in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU), majoring Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing. I am a student member of the Laboratory for Intelligence Interaction and Perception (IIP) supervised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree in Remote Sensing Science and Technology from Wuhan University in 2016.

My research interest includes computer vision and machine learning. And I have research experience on remote sensing image processing, computational vision such as saliency and human scanpath prediction. In addition, I am experienced in challenges or contests on multimedia analysis, including vehicle retrieval, pedestrian detection and tracking, abnormal event detection, android application development.

Currently, I focus on the 3D object detection and I am actively looking for Ph.D. positions in computer vision.
