About Me

I am currently a final-year Master candidate in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU), majoring in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing. I am a member of the Lab. of Intelligent Information Processing (IIP) supervised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen. Before that, I received a bachelor's degree from Wuhan University in 2016. My curriculum vitae is here: CV.

My research interests are related to computer vision and machine learning, including the topics of 2D/3D object detection, visual saliency, and remote sensing image processing. My future research topics will focus more on video analytics.

I will start my Ph.D. journey at the Rochester of Institute Technology (R.I.T) in fall 2019, under the guidance of Prof. Yu Kong.
