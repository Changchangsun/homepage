  • HSI Band Selection

    Band selection can give us interpretable insights to the later constructed spectral models and help reveal physical meanings behind compared with other dimention reduction methods for hyper spectral images (HSI) in remote sensing.

    I mainly focus on exploring optimal algorithm with machine learning methods to figure out the mechanism of band selection for remote sensing data.

    Computer Vision

    Computer vision has witnessed great progress with the help of deep learning techniques in recent years.

    I mainly focus on pedestrain detection task for surveillance video with deep neural networks, and rencently I has been developing a distributed deep learning framework on embedded Tegra & Jetson TK1 board, designed for face recognition related tasks and remote sensing big data applications.
