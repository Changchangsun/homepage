About Me

I am a Ph.D. student (2019-) at B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences (GCCIS) of Rochester Institute of Technology (R.I.T), supervised by Prof. Yu Kong and co-advised by Prof. Qi Yu. I received my Master's degree (2016-2019) and Bachelor's degree (2012-2016) both at the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU). During the years in WHU, I was a member of the Lab. of Intelligent Information Processing (IIP) supervised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen. My curriculum vitae is here: CV.

My research interests are related to computer vision and machine learning, including the topics of 2D/3D object detection, video anomaly detection, visual saliency, and remote sensing image processing.
