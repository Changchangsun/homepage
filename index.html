About Me

I am currently a final-year Master candidate in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU), majoring in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing. I am currently a member of the Lab. of Intelligent Information Processing (IIP) supervised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen. Before that, I received a bachelor's degree in Remote Sensing Science and Technology from Wuhan University in 2016. My curriculum vitae is here: CV.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. I have research experience on topics of object detection and tracking, remote sensing image processing, computational vision such as saliency and human scanpath prediction. In addition, I am experienced in challenges or contests on video analytics, including vehicle retrieval, pedestrian detection and tracking, abnormal event detection.

Currently, I focus on the 3D object detection for autonomous driving and I am actively looking for Ph.D. positions.
